Instruction for Patients
  1. Select "Account" in the navigation bar.
  2. Click "Sign Up" and input your details.
  3. Upon registeration, notify your clinician and provide your Identity Card number.
  4. The clinician will be able to "append" you into his/her list of patients.

  • For staff, your special accounts can only be generated by admins.
  • Contact the team (refer beneath) should the supposed creation of your account is delayed.
  • For clinicians, you may add patients using their Identity Card numbers.
