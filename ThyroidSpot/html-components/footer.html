Thyroid-SPOT

Fueled by a patented and clinically validated algorithm, Thyroid-SPOT is a one-stop website that serves as a bridge between patients dealing with thyroid-related illnesses and endocrinologists (clinicians). The site aids in analysis and allows both groups of users to stay organized and be au courant with the treatment progress.
